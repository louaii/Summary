Step 1: The standard way
Open the Start Menu and type Windows Security.

Go to App & browser control.

Click on Smart App Control settings.

Change the setting to Off.

If the option is greyed out, see the Registry fix below.

Step 2: The "Greyed Out" Registry Fix
If Windows won't let you click "Off" in the settings menu, you can force it through the Registry:

Press Win + R, type regedit, and hit Enter.

Navigate to:

HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\CI\Policy

Look for the value VerifiedAndReputablePolicyState.

Double-click it and change the Value Data to 0 (this means "Off").

Restart your computer. This is a critical step; the change won't apply until a full reboot.
